🌍 GulfParcels Marketplace Analytics

📌 Project Overview
GulfParcels is a data-driven project analyzing the feasibility of a peer-to-peer luggage logistics platform combined with a cultural marketplace connecting Gulf countries with Africa and South Asia.


🎯 Problem Statement

Logistics Challenges
- High cost of international cargo shipping
- Delays in traditional logistics systems
- Underutilization of airline luggage capacity

Marketplace Challenges
- Limited access to authentic home-country products
- High prices in retail stores
- Informal trade through unstructured channels

🔍 Limitations of Existing Solutions

Many users rely on platforms like Facebook and WhatsApp for coordination.

Key Issues:
- No identity verification → high fraud risk  
- No structured matching system  
- No pricing transparency  
- No secure payments  
- No data tracking  
- Limited scalability  


📊 Quantitative Insights (Initial Hypothesis)

- Estimated 30–50% unused luggage capacity per traveler  
- Average cargo cost: $6–$10 per kg  
- Informal transaction inefficiency: 20–40% failure rate  

Example Opportunity:
10 kg unused × $8/kg = $80 per traveler  


💡 Proposed Solution

A dual-platform enabling:

✈️ Peer-to-peer luggage transport  
🛒 Cultural product marketplace

- Compare logistics cost models  
- Identify high-demand routes  
- Evaluate marketplace demand
